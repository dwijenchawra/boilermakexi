Motion Descirption:
- The hand appears from the bottom of the screen
- Then the hand moves up and down, starting at the bottom, quickly moving up, and slowy moving down
- Finally the hand disappears off the botton of the screen

Hand Description:
- After appearing, the index finger shows when the hand is moving upward
- The index finger is hidden when the hand is moving downward

Summary:
- This is most similar to a scrolling gesture as the hand moves up and down with the index finger appearing and disappearing.
